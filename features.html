What is ACE English?

ACE English is a 15-day practical English workshop designed to help students improve speaking and writing skills through activities, interaction, and confidence-building tasks.

Improved communication skills
Stronger writing skills
Better vocabulary and sentence formation
Clear pronunciation and expression
